How are the scores calculated?

Synergy score teal — how well a suggested card fits your deck’s themes and roles:

{% if section == "new" %}

score = 2 × matched tags + 1 ÷ log10(EDHREC rank + 10)
More theme/role matches and a lower EDHREC rank both raise this number.

{% else %}

score = 2 × theme matches + 1.5 × role-gap fills + 1 ÷ log10(EDHREC rank + 10)
Cards that cover gaps in your deck’s role spread and rank well on EDHREC score highest.

{% endif %}

Replaceability score amber — how swappable a current deck card is (1–10):

score = role overlap weight + CMC saved bonus + quality adjustment
Higher means the card shares more roles with the suggestion and/or has a high CMC relative to what would replace it — making it a stronger swap-out candidate.

Heads up: These are algorithmic suggestions, not prescriptions. The tool weighs role overlap, theme tag coverage, CMC efficiency, and EDHREC popularity — but it cannot account for everything: specific combo lines, political utility, pet cards, budget constraints, regional card availability, power-level preferences, or subtle synergies that aren’t captured in role tags. A high replaceability score on a target card means the algorithm thinks it’s a reasonable swap candidate; it doesn’t mean you must cut it. Always apply your own judgment before making changes.
